Cat-Fight Kerfuffle

  • CONTROLS Arrow keys move left and right. Press the down arrow key to guard. Press space to punch.
  • High Concept: Boxing Doggy momentum Use timely boxing skills to repel doggy.
  • Player Experience Goals: Hardcore Are you a bad enough dude?
  • Genre: Brawling The mechanics are intended for the brawling genre.
  • Game play: The player controls the boxing-veteran cat and times punches (or guards) with the incoming enemy. Guarding prevents loss of health, but can only be performed as long as the player has stamina. Getting hit pushes the player back in such a distance proportionate to the enemy speed. The doggy moves faster as he takes damage.
  • Prototype goal: Hitbox and collision work.
  • Features: Reflex training.
  • Setting/Premise: The player controls a Cat with a troubled past. A doggy is lost in a field and his panic has hastened his pace. He wants to find his owners as quickly as possible, only you stand in his way.
  • Story: The dog is lost and scared, reaching for the strand of hope that he can be reunited with his loved ones. But you, the cat, know the truth. Stop him at all costs.
  • Target Audience: People who appreciate a reflex challenge.
  • Play time: Only a few minutes, expanding the longer it takes the player to master the game.
  • Strategies: Aim to have the punch linger while the dog gets close, then block the doggy when he travels at higher speed.
  • Prototype Assets: Phaser documents online.
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: Final Fight or River City Ransom.
